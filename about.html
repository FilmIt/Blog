About me

Melanie Moeller is a Creative Technologist, Business Intelligence Analyst and Digital Innovation Expert, currently based in the UK. Holding an MSc in Creative Technologies, she is passionate about creating contemporary and edgy technologies. With her company Media Production Company SOPHROSYNE PRODUCTIONS AND DIGITAL MEDIA she has produced promotional videos, documentaries and bespoke solutions for digital media. Currently, she is part of the BBC News Online team. As part of a multi-disciplicary team she plans and coordinates the implementation of new, cutting edge features and helps the News Online team to deliver world class journalism at scale. Her work also involves business requirements analysis and digital analytics including customer segmentation, multi-variate analysis, modelling, and path analysis.

A passion for innovative solutions let her to explores new emerging formats for News. In the past, Melanie developed a News application for Google Glass, has led an interactive video project exploring WireWAX tagging technology and new digital storytelling formats for BBC Connected Studio and has run 'Maker Sessions' enabling BBC News teams to prototype wearable gadgets and smart objects for newsgathering.

"I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of technology and creativity and enjoy creating innovative formats that engage audiences in new and unique ways."

Melanie is also the founder of FilmKit the UK's first Smartphone festival, which is not only a platform to share mobile films but also to discover innovative forms of filmmaking such as capturing footage with drones, virtual reality and interactive video.
